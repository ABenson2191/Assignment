MBTI Results

16 personality MBTI test

My result was Defender personality.
Defenders are practical and dedicated. Known for their reliability and altruism, Defenders are good at creating and maintaining a secure and stable environment for themselves and their loved ones. Defenders dedication is invaluable in many areas, including their own personal growth.
Defenders can be easily caught out in areas where their kindness and practical approach can be more a liability than an asset. Whether it is finding a partner, learning to relax or improvise, reaching dazzling heights on the career ladder, or managing their workload, Defenders need to put in a conscious effort to develop their weaker traits and additional skills.

Learning Style Test

Educational planner test

My Scores:
Auditory: 30%
Visual: 30%
Tactile: 40%
I am a Tactile learner which means I learn by touching and doing things

What do these results mean for me?

What these results have indicated to me is that I am hands on person. I learn best when I am playing around with things, instead of someone telling how to do it. I am a reserved person that is very loyal and demanding of myself. I also must push myself out of my comfort zone to achieve the things that I want in life. I am very driven and determined to achieve the goals I have set for myself.


How do I think these results will influence my behaviour in a team?

These results will influence me to be more proactive in my group. Not to just accept the norm but to push and challenge my team members in a positive way. Positive feedback pushes us all to be better and improve. My behavior in a team environment is very structured. I may find it frustrating if things are not completed by the dates set out by the group. The way I work is to be efficient with my time and when working in a team, everyone looks out for each other not themselves.


How should I take these result's into account when forming a team?

When forming a team, I need to consider a few aspects of my test results. I am a very visual person so when people are communicating or describing things to me visual aids or graphs are very good tools. I am someone who has been brought up in team environments through sport, so having the defender personality is not a shock for me. I need to remember that some people prefer to work alone and won’t be forth coming. Different personalities will clash but ultimately this assignment is a group assignment and people will have to find a way to work together.
