About Me

After completing year 12 at Tranby College, I completed an Apprenticeship in Refrigeration and
Airconditioning. I now work for a company called IT Critical Systems. At IT Critical we provide a total
cooling and power solution for Data Centers, Server Rooms and Industrial Control Systems.

I was Born in Scotland and have lived in Australia for 12 years. My partner and I are recently engaged.
We have bought our first house in Perth and have started to renovate our 1940’s-character home.

My interest in IT

With my work, I get to travel Australia and maintain data centers for mining operations. By placing these centers on the ground, it allows a central hub in a major city to run multiple mine site's miles away. It is fascinating. Autonomous trucks to loaders and stackers. Massive machinery all controlled by one person in a chair mile’s away. Working around this environment has sparked an interest for me and I can't wait to learn more.

IT experience

I use technology every day from Bluetooth in my tools to connecting my laptop to A/C units using HyperTerminal. I have some minor networking skills that I have gained through my time in my trade.

Why RMIT?

I chose RMIT because of two main reasons. One because of the reputation the university has built for itself. And two because I am able to complete a bachelor’s of IT without giving up my job that I love. I can advance my skills and become more desirable to my company.

What do I expect to learn?

This course will develop my skills and further my understanding of networking and programming. I expect to learn the principles of programming and be able to confidently write my own code. I am hoping this course will give me the skills required to bridge a gap between my trade and information technology.
